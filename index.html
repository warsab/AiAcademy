Full Transcript

0:00Welcome to the first lesson of Claude Code Mastery. In this module, we're going to explore the foundations of Anthropic — the company behind Claude. Understanding who built this technology and why is crucial to mastering it.

1:24Anthropic was founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei, along with several other researchers who previously worked at OpenAI. Their core mission centres on AI safety — building AI systems that are reliable, interpretable, and steerable.

3:15What sets Anthropic apart is their research-first approach. They published the Constitutional AI paper, which introduced a novel method for training AI assistants to be helpful, harmless, and honest without relying solely on human feedback for every interaction.

5:02In this lesson, we'll walk through the key milestones in Anthropic's journey, examine their safety-focused philosophy, and understand how this foundation directly impacts the way Claude behaves and responds.

7:30Let's start with a brief timeline. In 2021, the company was founded with a Series A funding round. By 2022, they had released their first Claude model. In 2023, Claude 2 launched with expanded capabilities, and by 2024, Claude 3 introduced the Opus, Sonnet, and Haiku model family — each optimised for different use cases.

10:15Understanding this evolution is important because each generation of Claude brought improvements not just in capability, but in alignment and safety. The techniques we'll learn in later modules — from prompt engineering to advanced API usage — are all built on top of these safety foundations.

12:00That wraps up our introduction. In the next lesson, we'll dive into the specific research papers that shaped Claude's development. See you there.
